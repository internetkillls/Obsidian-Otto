## Exploration patterns

### Source types in the vault

- Primary reflections (first-person, direct observation)
- Literature notes (paraphrased or quoted from readings)
- Argument maps (claims linked to sources)
- Evidence compilations (data or examples collected for a claim)
- Method notes (how something was done or designed)

### Source evaluation criteria

- First-person reflection: high weight for work style, friction, recovery
- Literature notes: cite the original source. Paraphrase, don't copy.
- Argument maps: verify that claims map to sources, not assumptions
- Evidence compilations: check for recency and representativeness
